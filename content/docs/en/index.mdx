Welcome. This wiki is written by creators, for creators. It walks you through making custom skins for League of Legends — modeling, VFX, animations, and the tools used by the Divine Skins community.

New here? Open **Start here** and follow the walkthrough. Already know your way around? Pick a topic on the left.

## Browse by topic

- [Start here](/docs/guided-walkthrough) — the beginner-to-advanced walkthrough.
- [Tools](/docs/tools) — LtMAO, CSLOL, Obsidian, and the rest of the stack.
- [Maya](/docs/maya) — rigging, skinning, and exporting for League.
- [Blender](/docs/blender) — modeling and exporting to League-friendly formats.
- [Animations](/docs/animations) — idle loops, recalls, and edits.
- [VFX & bins](/docs/vfx-bins) — particles, bin files, and visual effects.
- [Assets library](/docs/assets-library) — shared rigs, textures, and references.
- [Errors & fixes](/docs/errors) — common crashes and how to get past them.

## Want to help?

Every page on this wiki has an **Edit on GitHub** button. Spotted something wrong? Fix it. Got a guide in your head? Head to [/contribute](/contribute) and write it — the visual editor opens a pull request for you.
