## What is Blender?

**Blender** is a free and open-source 3D creation suite. It supports the entire 3D pipeline including:

- **Modeling** – Create and sculpt 3D meshes
- **Texturing** – UV unwrapping and texture painting
- **Rigging** – Build armatures and bone systems
- **Animation** – Keyframe and motion capture support
- **Rendering** – Cycles and EEVEE render engines

### Blender vs Maya for LoL Modding

While Blender is a powerful free alternative, **Maya is the preferred tool** in our modding community due to:

- **Better Plugin Support** – LoL-Maya provides the most complete workflow for League models
- **More Community Resources** – Most tutorials and guides are Maya-focused
- **Proven Workflow** – Established pipeline for importing, editing, and exporting

> **Recommendation:** If you're serious about LoL modding, we recommend [using Maya](/docs/maya/lol-maya-plugin) for the best experience. Blender can work for basic tasks but has limited plugin support.

### When to Use Blender

- You need a **free** 3D tool and can't access Maya
- You're doing **basic model viewing** or **texture work**
- You're already experienced with Blender and prefer it
