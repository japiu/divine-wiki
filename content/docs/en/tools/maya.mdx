## Getting Autodesk Maya (2023)

> ⚠️ **Important:** Make sure you're using **Maya 2023** for the best compatibility.
> - Maya 2024.1 is known to work.
> - Maya 2024.2 may have bugs and is not recommended.

If you're a student at a recognized school or university, you can get Maya free for up to **1 year**.

[Get Maya for Students](https://www.autodesk.com/education/free-software/maya)

## Paid Subscription

If you choose to purchase Maya, there are three subscription plans available:

| Plan     | Total Cost | Cost per Month |
|----------|------------|----------------|
| 3 Years  | $5,625     | $156.25        |
| 1 Year   | $1,875     | $156.25        |
| 1 Month  | $235       | $235           |

> 💡 Subscriptions are paid upfront, so the total amount is charged at once.
