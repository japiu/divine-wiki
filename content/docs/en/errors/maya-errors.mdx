## Multiple Materials
<img src="/wiki-images/1767145646958-37lcw.png" alt="Image" width={400} />

The best tip to separate materials is to go into **F11** (face selection mode) and only select the parts you want to be separated with a double click, then assign a new material.

---

## 4+ Influences

> "Too many influences" means a vertex is affected by more than 4 bones, which League of Legends doesn't support.

<img src="/wiki-images/1767145658279-q2fe1k.png" alt="Image" width={400} />

**To fix it:**
1. First click on the mesh
2. Then press the **"Auto fix 4 influences"** button in the LoL-Maya shelf

It will automatically limit each vertex to a max of 4 bone weights.
