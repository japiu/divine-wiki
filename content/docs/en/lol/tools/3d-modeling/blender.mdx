Blender is a free, open-source 3D suite. For a long time, Maya was the
only realistic choice for League modding. With the
[**Aventurine** plugin](/docs/lol/tools/3d-modeling/aventurine) by Bud and Frog, Blender
is now a real option.

[Download Blender](https://www.blender.org/download/)

---

## What you get with Blender

- Modeling and sculpting.
- UV unwrapping and texture painting.
- Rigging and skeletons.
- Keyframe animation.
- Cycles and EEVEE render engines.

---

## Blender vs Maya for League modding

Both work. Pick whichever you'll actually use.

| If you...                             | Pick                     |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Want a free, fully-featured option    | **Blender + Aventurine** |
| Already know Blender                  | **Blender + Aventurine** |
| Want the largest tutorial library     | **Maya + lol_maya**      |
| Have a Maya license or student access | **Maya + lol_maya**      |

The community still leans Maya for established rigging workflows, but
Aventurine has closed most of the gap.

---

## What you'll need

- Blender 4.0 or newer.
- The [Aventurine: League Tools](/docs/lol/tools/3d-modeling/aventurine) addon for SKN
  / SKL / ANM import and export.
