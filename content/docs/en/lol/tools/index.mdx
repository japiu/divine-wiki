These are all the tools we cover, with a one-line purpose for each. Click any
card for the full guide.

**New here?** Grab [Flint](/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/flint),
[Jade](/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/jade), and
[LtMAO](/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/ltmao) first. Those three cover most
of the work. The rest you'll pick up as you need them.

---

## Modding apps

The core toolkit: extracting, editing, packing, fixing, audio.

<ToolCard
  name="Flint"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/flint"
  badge="Recommended"
>
  All-in-one modding app. Extract, preview, edit BIN files, and pack the
  mod. The easiest start if you don't want to juggle separate tools.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Jade"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/jade"
  badge="Recommended"
>
  Visual BIN editor with syntax coloring, hover previews, and live hash
  lookups. Newer workflow than Ritobin.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="Quartz" href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/quartz">
  VFX and material editor. Paint mode for particle recoloring, Port for
  moving VFX between champions, RGBA helper, batch image recolor.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="LtMAO" href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/ltmao">
  Toolpack covering BIN, audio (`bnk_tool`, `wiwawe`), WAD packing, no-skin
  builder, animation fixes, and a built-in mod manager (`cslmao`).
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Hexbelt"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/hexbelt"
  badge="Web"
>
  Browser-based WAD and BIN viewer. No install. Files stay on your machine.
  Best for quick one-off lookups.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Ritobin"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/ritobin"
  badge="Older workflow"
>
  Drag-and-drop converter that turns `.bin` into editable text. Solid for
  batch scripts. Jade covers most cases now.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="Hematite" href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/hematite">
  Auto-detects and fixes common skin issues. Invisible HP bars, white
  models, missing icons, broken particles.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="League Mod Repather"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/league-mod-repather"
>
  Rebuilds an old mod for the current patch when a League update breaks it.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Wwise"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/wwise"
  badge="Advanced"
>
  Pro audio middleware. Only needed if you're rebuilding full `.bnk`
  soundbanks. Free tier covers hobby projects.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Manifest Downloader GUI"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/modding-apps/manifest-downloader-gui"
>
  Downloads Riot manifest files. Useful for older patches, PBE, or missing
  assets.
</ToolCard>

## 3D modeling

For champion models, rigging, and animations.

<ToolCard name="Maya" href="/docs/lol/tools/3d-modeling/maya">
  Industry standard 3D software. Best for rigging and UVs. Pair with the
  [lol_maya](https://github.com/tarngaina/lol_maya) plugin. Paid.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Blender"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/3d-modeling/blender"
  badge="Free"
>
  Free, open-source 3D suite. A real option for League now thanks to the
  Aventurine plugin.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Aventurine"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/3d-modeling/aventurine"
  badge="Divine team"
>
  Blender addon by Bud and Frog. Imports and exports League meshes,
  skeletons, and animations. Adds hair / cloth physics and animation
  retargeting.
</ToolCard>

## Texturing

Image editors, plugins to open League's `.tex` files, and Windows Explorer
previews.

<ToolCard name="Photoshop" href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/photoshop">
  Industry standard image editor. Best for serious texture work. Paid.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard name="GIMP" href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/gimp" badge="Free">
  Free open-source image editor. The closest thing to a free Photoshop.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Paint.NET"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/paint-net"
  badge="Free"
>
  Lightweight Windows image editor. The simplest option if Photoshop and
  GIMP feel like overkill.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Photoshop TEX Plugin"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/photoshop-tex-plugin"
>
  Adds `.tex` open and save support to Photoshop. Required for League
  texture work in Photoshop.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="GIMP Tex Plugin"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/gimp-tex-plugin"
>
  Adds `.tex` open and save support to GIMP.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Paint.NET Tex Plugin"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/paint-net-tex-plugin"
>
  Adds `.tex` open and save support to Paint.NET.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="Tex Thumbnail Provider"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/tex-thumbnail-provider"
>
  Shows `.tex` files as image thumbnails in Windows Explorer. By GuiSai.
</ToolCard>
<ToolCard
  name="LTK Tex Thumb Handler"
  href="/docs/lol/tools/texturing/ltk-tex-thumb-handler"
>
  Same idea as the GuiSai handler, from the LeagueToolkit team. Pick one,
  not both.
</ToolCard>
